Welcome to my kitchen and get ready for some baking fun! My name is Emma and since 2011, I’ve tested, written, photographed, and published more than 1,200 from-scratch recipes on my website and in my best-selling cookbooks.

I Know The Importance of a Trusted Recipe

Through my recipes and helpful baking tips & tutorials, I’ve helped millions of bakers gain the knowledge and confidence to bake from scratch. Before I share a new recipe, you can feel confident that my team and I have tested it dozens of times in our home kitchens.

This is why Emma’s Baking Addiction has become a trusted resource for bakers around the world.
